A little bit about me...

I'm graphic designer located in Texas who loves turning ideas into visuals that grab attention and communicate clearly. I’ve built experience creating magazine layouts, websites, and packaging that align with visual identities and tell compelling stories. For me, everything is a story waiting to be expressed through color, shape, and line. These elements transform information into something visually readable and engaging. My passion for understanding data has led me to pursue education in GIS, combining my design expertise with spatial data to explore new ways of visual storytelling. I’m excited to continue to grow as a person and professional–- and I can’t wait what projects lie ahead. Take a look at my work!
