The Mag: Editorial Design

Project Overview

The Mag is a quarterly lifestyle magazine created for LSU students, featuring campus culture, stories, and local favorites. Each issue reaches about 5,000 readers across campus, plus a special edition for incoming freshmen. I collaborated with writers, photographers, and marketers to bring each publication to life, contributing to the visual design and overall layout.

The challenge

Each issue had its own balance of text and visuals, and sometimes we had more copy than photography. The challenge was keeping every spread visually engaging and easy to read while staying true to the fun, student-centered tone of the magazine.

What I did

To make the layouts more dynamic, I focused on typography, grid systems, and visual hierarchy to guide the reader’s eye. I added illustrations and photography to break up dense content and bring energy to the pages. By adjusting contrast, spacing, and rhythm throughout the design, I kept each issue consistent, approachable, and fun to flip through.
